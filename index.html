How to:
Strength is the parameter responsible for the strength of the color change in one direction or another. By default: in the center = equal to zero.
Step 1:
Select the hue by moving the triangle markers on the hue strip.
Step 2:
Select the strength by moving the circle inside Color Picker rectangle.
Step 3:
Select the base color by moving the slider.
Step 4:
While choosing a color in four additional blocks is calculated an optimal set of colors in real-time.
